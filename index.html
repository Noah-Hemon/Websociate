Welcome to JamCat

This project was created by Noah HEMON and Ian ROUVRAIS. JamCat is a real-time quiz game application where you can either join a game room to participate in quizzes or create your own quizzes to challenge others.

To get started:

  • Click "Play Game" to join an existing game room and test your knowledge against other players.
  • Click "Create Quiz" to design your custom quiz and share it with others.

Enjoy the game and have fun learning!
